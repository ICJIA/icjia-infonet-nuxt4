  services—minutes or hours?
answer: Use .25 hour increments. 15 minutes = .25 hours; 30 minutes = .5 hours;

  of time spent with the client providing service and time completing
  documentation for that interaction.

Use .25 hour increments. 15 minutes = .25 hours; 30 minutes = .5 hours; etc. The time entered should reflect as closely as possible the total amount of time spent with the client providing service and time completing documentation for that interaction.
