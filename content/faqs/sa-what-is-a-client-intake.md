A client intake is information collected to document the client’s profile at the time of intake into the program. Sexual assault service provider agencies are required to use ICASA-approved intake forms. Sample forms may be obtained from ICASA or at https://icjia.illinois.gov. 

In addition, agencies may create their own data collection forms and request ICASA approval before use.
