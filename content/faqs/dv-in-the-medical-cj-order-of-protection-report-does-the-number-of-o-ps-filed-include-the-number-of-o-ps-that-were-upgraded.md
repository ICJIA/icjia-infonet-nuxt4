question: In the Medical/CJ Order of Protection report, does the number of OPs
  filed include the number of OPs that were upgraded?
answer: >-
  Numbers of Orders of Protection filed (by status) and OPs issued (by type)
  during the reporting period are displayed in the first section of the report
  (New Orders of Protection), whether they were subsequently upgraded or not. 


  Numbers of existing OPs that were upgraded during the reporting period are displayed in the second section of the report by the type of upgrade (Upgrade in the Type of Order).

Numbers of Orders of Protection filed (by status) and OPs issued (by type) during the reporting period are displayed in the first section of the report (New Orders of Protection), whether they were subsequently upgraded or not. 

Numbers of existing OPs that were upgraded during the reporting period are displayed in the second section of the report by the type of upgrade (Upgrade in the Type of Order).
