question: If adult clients attend a Children’s Group Counseling session with
  their children on a limited basis to help understand what their child is
  experiencing, how should I document that activity for the adult? For the
  children?
answer: Document the parent’s attendance in Parental Services. Document the
  group session under the children’s records as Group Children’s Counseling.
