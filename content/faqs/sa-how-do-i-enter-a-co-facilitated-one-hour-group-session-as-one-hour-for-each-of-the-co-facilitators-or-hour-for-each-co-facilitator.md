question: How do I enter a co-facilitated, one-hour group session as one hour
  for each of the co-facilitators or ½ hour for each co-facilitator?
answer: >-
  Go to **Services > Group Services** and enter the group session indicating the
  Conduct Hours for each staff person on the right side of the screen. 


  If both staff co-facilitated during the whole session, both staff should receive one Conduct Hour. Remember, total session hours documented for staff should match the length of the session (one hour).

Go to **Services > Group Services** and enter the group session indicating the Conduct Hours for each staff person on the right side of the screen. 

If both staff co-facilitated during the whole session, both staff should receive one Conduct Hour. Remember, total session hours documented for staff should match the length of the session (one hour).
