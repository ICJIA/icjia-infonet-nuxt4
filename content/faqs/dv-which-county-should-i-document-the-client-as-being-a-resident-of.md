question: Which county should I document the client as being a resident of?
answer: The county in which she resided at the time of intake. If your client
  lives in Christian County and moves to shelter in Sangamon County shortly
  after intake, the client is still considered a Christian County client.

  should be documented in residences.

The county in which she resided at the time of intake. If your client lives in Christian County and moves to shelter in Sangamon County shortly after intake, the client is still considered a Christian County client. However, if the clients move to Sangamon County (and if applicable move back) should be documented in residences.
