question: How do I enter a one-hour group session co-facilitated by two
  staff—one hour or ½ hour for each co-facilitator?
answer: Go to ServicesGroup Services and enter the group session indicating the
  Conduct Hours and Preparation Hours for each staff person. If both staff
  co-facilitated during the whole session, both staff should receive one full
  Conduct Hour. Remember, Total Session Hours should match the length of the
  session (one hour).

Go to ServicesGroup Services and enter the group session indicating the Conduct Hours and Preparation Hours for each staff person. If both staff co-facilitated during the whole session, both staff should receive one full Conduct Hour. Remember, Total Session Hours should match the length of the session (one hour).
