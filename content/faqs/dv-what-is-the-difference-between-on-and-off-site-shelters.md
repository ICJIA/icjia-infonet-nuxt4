question: What is the difference between on- and off-site shelters?
answer: On-Site Shelter is provided on-site at the residential domestic violence
  program. Off-site shelter is emergency shelter provided to clients by the
  domestic violence programs, but at an off-site location such at a safe home or
  motel.
