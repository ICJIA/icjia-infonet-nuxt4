Counseling services are the general emotional support, education, and other information and resource services that domestic violence programs regularly provide. Therapy activities are more intensive, therapeutic services provided by a licensed therapist working for the domestic violence program. If licensed staff are providing therapy, therapy categories (e.g. Group Therapy, Individual Therapy) should be used.
