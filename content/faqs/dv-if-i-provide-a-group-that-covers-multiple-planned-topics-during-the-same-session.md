Complete 1 group log sheet for the session indicating the amount of time spent on each topic. For example, if the session was 2 hours long and 1 hour of it was Adult Group Counseling, ½ hour was spent on Life Skills, and ½ hour was spent on Conflict Resolution, enter 3 separate group sessions: 1-hour session Adult Group Counseling; ½ hour session on Life Skills; and ½ hour session on Conflict Resolution.
