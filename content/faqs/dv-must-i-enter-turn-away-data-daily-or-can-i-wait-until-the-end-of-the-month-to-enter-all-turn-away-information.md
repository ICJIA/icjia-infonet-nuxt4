question: Must I enter turn away data daily or can I wait until the end of the
  month to enter all turn away information?
answer: You may wait until the end of the month to enter all turn away data at
  the same time. However, consider that you will not be able to extract daily
  turn away information from InfoNet. If you enter monthly, you will only be
  able to extract monthly totals on the turn away report. Additionally, monthly
  data entry will remove the ability to know the number of families turned away;
  only the numbers of adults and children turned away can be reported.

You may wait until the end of the month to enter all turn away data at the same time. However, consider that you will not be able to extract daily turn away information from InfoNet. If you enter monthly, you will only be able to extract monthly totals on the turn away report. Additionally, monthly data entry will remove the ability to know the number of families turned away; only the numbers of adults and children turned away can be reported.
