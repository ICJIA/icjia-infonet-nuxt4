question: If a child was a client of the program and returns as an adult victim,
  do we assign her a new client ID number as if she were a new client?
answer: If your client was originally seen as a child witness and returns as an
  adult, primary victim of domestic violence, close their child file, and open
  him or her as an adult with a new client ID number. Reference this client’s
  history with the program in their physical file.

If your client was originally seen as a child witness and returns as an adult, primary victim of domestic violence, close their child file, and open him or her as an adult with a new client ID number. Reference this client’s history with the program in their physical file.
