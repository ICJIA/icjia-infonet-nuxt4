You would enter the group service as Group IDVA, 1 session for 1 hour, and then all three (3) Client ID numbers on the Attendees tab. This will show up on the Standard Services Report in multiple places depending on which sub-report you are viewing: 1) On the Client Services Report (1st table), 3 hours of direct client service will be displayed because 3 clients each received 1 hour of service and 2) 1 hour of service will be displayed on the Community, Institutional, & Group Services Report, because the session was 1 hour (Presentation Hours).
