Yes, but remember that as information you want to track becomes more specific and detailed, the more data entry is required. Before adding a very specific activity to your Lookup List, ask yourself how knowing this information in the future will benefit the program. If you can answer  this  question,  then  it  may be  worthwhile  to  spend  extra time tracking the information. Programs should always consider benefits of having more data in addition to the amount of extra resources that will be required to not only collect but also use that data when making these decisions.
