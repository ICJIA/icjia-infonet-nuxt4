question: When a young client turns 18 while in shelter, is she reentered as an
  18- year-old adult?
answer: If the child only remained in shelter for a couple of months after her
  18th  birthday, then she probably would not need an adult file. If she
  continues to receive services for several months after her birthday, it may be
  more appropriate to close her child file and open an adult file for her.

If the child only remained in shelter for a couple of months after her 18th  birthday, then she probably would not need an adult file. If she continues to receive services for several months after her birthday, it may be more appropriate to close her child file and open an adult file for her.
