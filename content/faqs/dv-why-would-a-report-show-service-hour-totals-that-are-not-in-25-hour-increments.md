question: Why would a report show service hour totals that are not in .25 hour
  increments?
answer: This should only occur if a Funding Filter is applied to a report. For
  example, if a VAWA grant pays for 33% of a staff person’s service time, a
  Standard Service Report with a VAWA filter will display .33 hours for every
  hour of service this person provided. If you are seeing this even when no
  funding filters are applied, please contact the ICJIA help desk for assistance
  at 312-793-8550 or  CJA.InfonetHelp@illinois.gov.

This should only occur if a Funding Filter is applied to a report. For example, if a VAWA grant pays for 33% of a staff person’s service time, a Standard Service Report with a VAWA filter will display .33 hours for every hour of service this person provided. If you are seeing this even when no funding filters are applied, please contact the ICJIA help desk for assistance at 312-793-8550 or  CJA.InfonetHelp@illinois.gov.
