question: Of which county should I document the client as being a resident?
answer: The county in which the client resides at the time of intake. For
  example, if your client is from Christian County but moves to Sangamon County
  upon intake, you would document Sangamon County as the client’s county of
  residence, not Christian County.

The county in which the client resides at the time of intake. For example, if your client is from Christian County but moves to Sangamon County upon intake, you would document Sangamon County as the client’s county of residence, not Christian County.
