question: How do I document Family Counseling in both the adult and child clients’ files?
answer: >-
  Document Family Counseling sessions in **Services > Group Services** for both
  the victim and significant others files, no matter whether the victim is an
  adult or a child. 


  Each significant other who receives Family Counseling should be a client entered into InfoNet as a significant other, even if family counseling is the only service received by a significant other.

Document Family Counseling sessions in **Services > Group Services** for both the victim and significant others files, no matter whether the victim is an adult or a child. 

Each significant other who receives Family Counseling should be a client entered into InfoNet as a significant other, even if family counseling is the only service received by a significant other.
