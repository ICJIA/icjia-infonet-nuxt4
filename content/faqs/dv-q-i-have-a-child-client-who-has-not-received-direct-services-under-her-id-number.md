question: Q I have a child client who has not received direct services under her
  ID number, but she has been in child care at the program while her mother
  receives services. Do I close her case?
answer: No, keep the case open. However, depending on the age of the child, the
  agency should consider whether the child may benefit from more meaningful
  interaction with staff such as counseling while the parent is receiving
  services.

No, keep the case open. However, depending on the age of the child, the agency should consider whether the child may benefit from more meaningful interaction with staff such as counseling while the parent is receiving services.
