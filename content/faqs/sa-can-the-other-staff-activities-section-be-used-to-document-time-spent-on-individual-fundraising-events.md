question: Can the Other Staff Activities section be used to document time spent
  on individual fundraising events?

  from this extra effort.

It’s possible to break out the data by each fundraising event by using the List Editor, but the number of activities you may assign in the drop down list is limited. Therefore, you may be limiting yourself for other staff activities in this section. Programs should also consider the tediousness of data collection/data entry of such detailed work vs. the benefit you will see from this extra effort.
