question: Is time spent entering client data and service data into InfoNet
  considered a direct client service?
answer: >-
  If you wish to track no shows and cancellations for services, you may do this

If you wish to track no shows and cancellations for services, you may do this on the **Services > Direct Client Services > No Show/Cancellation** tab. 

Do not document hours of service for the time you waited for the client if a service is not provided (i.e. client doesn’t show up at court). Simply enter a No Show record and make a note in the client’s paper file.
