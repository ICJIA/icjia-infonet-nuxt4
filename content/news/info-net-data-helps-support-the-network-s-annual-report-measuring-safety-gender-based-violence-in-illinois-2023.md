Each year, [The Network](https://the-network.org/) releases a comprehensive report on the recent trends in services, needs, and systems response to gender-based violence across Illinois. For 2023, The Network divided their report into four volumes, highlighting data on the Illinois Domestic Violence Hotline, Survivor Fund, domestic gun violence, and provisions of gender-based violence services in the state. ICJIA provided The Network with aggregate statewide and regional InfoNet data on clients and services provided by domestic violence and sexual assault providers. The full Measuring Safety 2023 Report can be viewed [here](https://the-network.org/measuring-safety-2023-download/), and Volume 3: Gender-Based Violence Services can be viewed [here](https://the-network.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/MeasuringSafety_GBV-PROVIDERS_2023_Digital.pdf).
