April is [Sexual Assault Awareness Month](https://nsvrc.org/saam) (SAAM). SAAM raises awareness about the extent of sexual violence and educates communities about preventing sexual violence and help-seeking.  

InfoNet data show that in 2023, the 32 sexual violence service providers that use InfoNet across Illinois served 10,593 individuals who either experienced sexual violence directly (92%) or were a significant other (i.e., family member, intimate partner, friend) of a sexual violence survivor (8%). Among these clients, more than a third were minors aged 17 or younger (36%); 14% identified as lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, or another sexual minority (LGBTQ+); 13% reported some type of physical, developmental, or mental/emotional disability; and 5% had limited or no English-speaking proficiency. In addition, service providers responded to 9,582 requests for anonymous crisis intervention via a hotline or in-person from survivors themselves or a family member or friend.
